airPiano - ctp431 final project 2018

Have you ever wanted to wave your hands around to make music? Well here with airPiano you nearly can!

This project wants to enable any user to improvise music while having simple to use and intuitive rules.

Instructions:

Leap Motion:

To use leapMotion first check that web app are permitted in the general tab of the leap motion control center.

Chords: put your left hand first ontop of the leap motion controller so it becomes the chord-playing hand. bring your index to your thumb to play a chord. The chord played is decided by which coloured plane your hand is above. The height of the hand also determines the speed of the arpeggio.

Melody: bring your right hand ontop of the leap motion controller after the first hand is displayed. Bring either your index, middle or ring finger to you thumb to produce a note. If the background is blue then higher notes will play, if it is orange then lower notes will play. Change this by moving your melody hand up or down.

Keyboard and Mouse:

Chords: click on one of the colours to play a chord. The height of the chord determines the speed of the arpeggio.

Melody: press 'a', 's' or 'd' to play lower notes, and 'q', 'w' or 'e' to play higher notes

Have fun!
